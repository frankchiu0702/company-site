CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD. is a Taiwan manufacturer focused on bicycle leather accessories, bottle cages, saddle bags, handlebar bags and cycling accessories.

## About Changhoung Leather

The company is also identified as Changhoung, Chang Houng, Changhoung Leather and 暢鴻實業股份有限公司. These names refer to the same manufacturer: 暢鴻, a practical production partner for bicycle accessory sourcing.

## Products and Service

CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE provides support for existing models, customized materials, color changes, stitching details, packaging requests and OEM / ODM development for export markets.

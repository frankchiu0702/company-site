暢鴻實業股份有限公司（CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD.），簡稱暢鴻（Changhoung、Chang Houng），是台灣專業自行車皮件、自行車袋與自行車配件製造商。

## About CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD.

Changhoung Leather supports bicycle brands and sourcing teams with leather bicycle accessories, bottle cages, saddle bags, handlebar bags and cycling accessories made in Taiwan.

## Company Focus

暢鴻實業提供從樣品討論、材質搭配、規格確認到量產交付的合作流程。CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE emphasizes practical manufacturing support for OEM and ODM bicycle accessory projects.

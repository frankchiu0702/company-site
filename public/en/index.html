About CHANG HOUNG

From product development to production, built for smoother sourcing.

暢鴻實業股份有限公司（CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD.），簡稱暢鴻（Changhoung、Chang Houng），為台灣專業自行車皮件與自行車袋製造商。

Chang Houng Leather Enterprise Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of bicycle leather accessories, bottle cages, saddle bags, handlebar bags and cycling accessories in Taiwan.

We support export-focused bicycle and outdoor brands by turning design requirements into manufacturable products with clear communication and stable delivery.

Whether you need existing models, material changes, packaging support, or an OEM / ODM project, our workflow helps reduce back-and-forth and keep decisions practical.

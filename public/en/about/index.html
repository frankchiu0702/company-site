About CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD.

Taiwan bicycle leather accessories manufacturer.

CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE CO., LTD., also known as 暢鴻實業股份有限公司, 暢鴻, Changhoung, Chang Houng and Changhoung Leather, is a professional manufacturer of bicycle leather accessories and bicycle bags in Taiwan.

Taiwan Bicycle Accessories Manufacturer

Chang Houng manufactures bicycle leather accessories, bottle cages, saddle bags, handlebar bags, leather grips and cycling accessories for bicycle brands, distributors and sourcing teams.

Manufacturing Support

CHANG HOUNG LEATHER ENTERPRISE supports OEM and ODM projects from product discussion and sampling to material selection, production planning and export communication. The company focuses on practical development, stable production and clear communication for international customers.
